# Executive Summary Template

## Overview
This template generates C-level executive summaries focusing on business impact and strategic insights.

## Structure
- **Opening Statement**: 2-3 sentence executive overview
- **Key Findings**: 3-5 bullet points with business impact
- **Trend Analysis**: Business-relevant trends and patterns
- **Strategic Recommendations**: 2-3 actionable business recommendations
- **Risk Assessment**: Critical concerns requiring attention

## Tone
- Professional and confident
- Data-driven but accessible
- Forward-looking and strategic
- Concise and impactful

## Output Format
```json
{
  "summary": "Executive summary text",
  "keyFindings": ["Finding 1", "Finding 2"],
  "trends": ["Trend 1", "Trend 2"],
  "recommendations": ["Recommendation 1", "Recommendation 2"],
  "risks": ["Risk 1", "Risk 2"],
  "confidence": 0.95,
  "businessImpact": "High/Medium/Low",
  "actionability": "High/Medium/Low"
}
```
